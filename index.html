Jen

Unity
C#

Jen is a turn-based strategy game I developed using Unity and C#. Set on a grid-based board, players command units made up of Jenga-like blocks. Players can also engage in diplomatic actions such as trade or armistice. The goal is to be the last remaining player on the board. I plan to make Jen available to the public, but before its release, I intend to train an AI model capable of playing against human opponents.
